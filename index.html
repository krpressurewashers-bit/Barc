Welcome to Barcelona

Barcelona is one of the most exciting cities in Europe because it combines beautiful beaches, historic buildings, and amazing culture. Visitors can explore famous locations such as the Sagrada Familia, walk through Las Ramblas, and see the colorful art and architecture designed by Antoni Gaudí. The city is also known for its incredible food, soccer culture, and lively street markets that attract travelers from around the world. A trip to Barcelona offers unforgettable views, historic landmarks, and unique experiences that make it an amazing destination for travelers.

Travel Itinerary

Day 1

Flight from Dallas to Barcelona with layover in Newark and check into Hotel El Palace Barcelona.

Day 2

Visit the Sagrada Familia and explore Las Ramblas street market. Enjoy local tapas for lunch.

Day 3

Spend time at Barcelona Beach, visit Park Güell, and see Gaudí’s colorful mosaics.

Day 4

Tour the Gothic Quarter, visit the Picasso Museum, and shop at La Boqueria Market.

Day 5

Return flight from Barcelona back to Dallas via Newark.
