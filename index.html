Barcelona is an awesome city to visit because there are so many fun things to do. You can check out the huge Sagrada Familia church, walk around Las Ramblas, and hang out at the beach. The city has really cool art by Antoni Gaudí and tasty food everywhere you go. I think anyone who likes exploring and seeing new stuff will have a great time in Barcelona.
